A Driver can get in a Car so that he can go somewhere.
 * Driver has to open the door to get in
 * Driver has to unlock a locked door before he can open it.

A Driver can lock and unlock the doors so that no one can steal his car.
 * Needs a key.

A Passenger can get into a Car so that he can go somewhere with the Driver.
 * Passengers have to open the door to enter.
 * Passengers can't open the doors if they're locked.
 * Up to X Passengers can ride in the Car safely.

A Driver can turn on a Car so that he can go somewhere.
 * Until he turns it on, a Car can't go anywhere
 * Driver needs a key to turn the Car on.

 A Driver can drive a Car so that he can get somewhere.
